About us

"Furniture" – work directly with furniture factories so that we can offer our customers furniture, mattresses, textiles and fabrics and any accessories on our website at the lowest prices from manufacturers. Huge range of products. We have over 100 brands of products. Over 20,000 samples of various furniture will allow each buyer to find the best option for themselves following their preferences and financial capabilities.

All products are carefully checked by our specialists, so we guarantee the quality and answer to the buyer for each product purchased by him. The presence of our own delivery service, thanks to which your goods can be delivered in Kyiv on the same day when the order is made. We will deliver your order within 1-5 days to any city or locality in Ukraine in the most convenient way for the client (Intime, Railway, New Mail, etc.).

Flexible store policy. We are ready to meet the needs of our customers and resolve all disputes for the buyer's benefit. Our store is officially open. We issue checks, pay taxes and fulfil all our obligations. We are ready to refund your money if there are any problems with the delivery of the goods by the Consumer Protection Law. We employ only highly qualified, efficient and polite employees.
